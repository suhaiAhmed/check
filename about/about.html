About Us

Discover Fovea, where a team of trailblazing experts embraces AI and computer vision to drive transformative solutions that shape the future.

Our History

In 2004, our journey began with pioneering neural network research in Japan, setting the stage for the innovative path we would follow. The culmination of years of dedication led to the completion of a PhD in cortical mechanisms and bio-inspired vision systems in 2014, solidifying our expertise in the field.

A pivotal moment arrived in 2018 when we published a groundbreaking research paper outlining the revolutionary concept of the sharp vision. This accomplishment, along with our vision, led to the establishment of Fovea Technology LLC in 2019, marked by the filing of an international patent. Momentum built as we secured seed funding from an angel investor in 2022, forming a talented and dedicated core team.

Today, in 2023, we are actively engaged in discussions with the UAE government, laying the foundation for potential manufacturing partnerships that will further advance our mission of innovation, efficiency, and safety.

Who We Are?

Fovea: Pioneering AI and Computer Vision Solutions

Fovea is an esteemed AI and computer vision company headquartered in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Founded in 2019 by a group of accomplished entrepreneurs and distinguished researchers, our firm specializes in creating cutting-edge AI solutions and advanced technologies.

Our Vision

Our vision is to elevate organizations' efficiency and security through advanced real-time analytics and alert systems. We're committed to integrating cutting-edge technology to empower businesses, ensuring they can make data-driven decisions while maintaining a robust security posture.

Our Mission

Fovea's mission is to transform safety and efficiency with our state-of-the-art Sharp Vision camera system. We harness advanced AI and deep learning tech to deliver real-time analytics and actionable alerts. Join us in redefining the future of security and performance

Meet Our Founders

Discover the brilliant minds behind Fovea's success

Our founders are the driving force behind Fovea, bringing a wealth of expertise in AI and computer vision technology. Get to know these visionary leaders who are shaping the future of Fovea and pioneering transformative solutions for businesses worldwide.

Dr. Abdul Rahman

CEO Founder

Abdul Rahman A.G., the Founder of Fovea Technology, combines academic prowess with practical expertise. Armed with an MSc in Artificial Intelligence and a PhD in System Design Engineering from the University of Fukui, Japan, he delved into research. He made notable contributions by utilizing complex-valued deep neural networks in a seismic reflection imaging classification project at the Petroleum Institute in Abu Dhabi. Later, at Universal Robot Co., Tokyo, he sharpened human palm ID spoof detection skills and extended his research as a Project Researcher at the Matsuo Lab, University of Tokyo, resulting in international journal publications and patents for practical computer vision applications.

From 2019 to 2022, Abdul Rahman led diverse projects as the Lead Computer Vision Engineer at Exawizards Inc., spanning neural network R&D, spatial vision calculation, 3D reconstruction, pipeline design, and multi-camera tracking. Today, he guides Fovea Technology, an Abu Dhabi-based startup established in 2019, specializing in cutting-edge AI solutions and technologies

Dr. Nishanth Koganti, CTO of Fovea Technology, is a seasoned AI engineer specializing in Robotics, Computer Vision, and Reinforcement Learning. With a PhD in Information Science from Nara Institute of Science and Technology, he rose from AI Engineer to CEO at Exawizards Inc.’s Indian subsidiary and previously excelled as a Senior Data Scientist at GEP Worldwide, addressing logistics and supply chain challenges.

Dr. Koganti’s remarkable career includes receiving the Best Application Paper Award at the IEEE IROS Conference in 2015, among 950 submissions. In his pivotal role at Fovea, he directs the company’s technical growth, leveraging his expertise in Computer Vision and Robotics to drive innovation.
